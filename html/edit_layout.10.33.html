Renaming a layout theme
You can rename custom themes that you create.
To rename a theme:
1.
In Layout mode, click Inspector in the layout bar, then click Appearance.
2.
Switch to a layout that uses the theme you want to rename.
3.
For Theme, click or and choose Rename Theme.
4.
Type a new name, then click OK.
Note  You can’t undo your last action after you rename a theme.
