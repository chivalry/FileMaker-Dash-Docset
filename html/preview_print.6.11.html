Printing troubleshooting
If you’re having trouble printing, look at the document in Preview mode. Click Preview in the layout bar (Browse mode), or choose Preview from the Mode pop-up menu at the bottom of the document window. To see all pages of the document, be sure to click the left and right arrows in the book.
